Hodophile Guard

Concept individual project where I was tasked to create a travel app. In a saturated market, developed an app that will be remembered.

See Project

Problem Statement

Users seek a reliable travel app that caters to various budgets, offering transportation, accommodations, and excursions for both solo and group travellers, with a focus on safety.


Problem Solution

The goal of the Hodophile Guard is to seamlessly connect like-minded travellers, ensuring cost-effective and secure group accommodations transportation and curated excursions, while prioritizing safety through criminal record checks and immunizations verifications.

Onboarding

The onboarding process in an app guides new users through its features and functionalities with a series of interactive tutorials and prompts, ensuring they quickly understand how to use the app effectively.

Matching Users

The app's matching algorithm intelligently pairs users with others based on shared interests, preferences, and activity history, enhancing user engagement and satisfaction.

Date Selection

The date selection process in the app allows users to easily choose preferred dates from an interactive calendar, ensuring a seamless and efficient scheduling experience.

Destination Matching

The destination matching process in the app suggests optimal travel locations based on user preferences, past behavior, and personalized criteria, enhancing the relevance and enjoyment of the recommendations.

Verifications

The safety verification process in the app ensures user authenticity and security by implementing thorough identity checks, including document verification and real-time photo validation.

Congratulations

The confirmation and congratulation process in the app provides users with a clear acknowledgment of their completed actions.
